About Me

Hi! I'm Julia, a full-stack web developer from Plano, TX.

I started out in User Experience Design, as I've always been passionate about ease-of-use and digital customer experiences. I had done a little bit of coding here and there, but it wasn't until I started working on a development team that I really started learning and enjoying code. I started helping the dev team with various projects, and quickly realized that coding was a passion of mine.

I didn't want to stop there. I was really curious about back-end development, databases, and front-end libraries/frameworks like React and Vue. I stumbled across SMU's Full Stack Web Development Boot Camp, and enrolled in February 2019. This boot camp gave me the foundation I needed to build full stack web applications, and I'm looking forward to using my new skills and pursuing a career in full stack development.
